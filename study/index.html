How this works
01 — Learn

Read the concept

Each concept is a page in this site, with diagrams and interactive demos you can poke at. Claude writes these — they live in study/.

02 — Build

Build it yourself

Every concept ends with a checkpoint: a real thing to build in builder/. You write all of it. Claude never does — that is the whole point.

03 — Loop

Come back & review

When your checkpoint runs, return. Claude reviews your work, checks you understand the why, then unlocks the next concept.

The milestones

Six milestones from empty folder to a deployable platform. Concepts unlock one at a time — you can't skip ahead, and that's deliberate.
